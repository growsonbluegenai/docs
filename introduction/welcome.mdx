## Why use BlueGenAI

BlueGenAI is designed to fundamentally improve how public-sector software is delivered.

- **Go from requirements to deliverables faster**

  Generate structured SDLC artifacts—including requirements, user stories, workflows, and data models—from stakeholder input and existing documentation.
- **Reduce delivery risk and rework**

  Standardize how teams translate business needs into technical designs and implementation plans, reducing ambiguity and costly downstream changes.
- **Accelerate build and integration**

  Produce implementation-ready outputs such as UI scaffolding, APIs, database schemas, and integration contracts that fit directly into your delivery workflow.
- **Maintain governance without slowing down**

  Support approvals, auditability, change control, and separation of duties—so speed doesn’t come at the expense of compliance.

## Who BlueGenAI is for

BlueGenAI is used by teams delivering modernization programs across federal, state, and local government environments.

### **Government systems integrators & delivery teams**

- Program delivery leads, solution architects, and engineering managers
- Teams implementing platforms and building custom applications
- Pods that need repeatable SDLC outputs and consistent delivery quality

### **Government agencies & internal IT teams**

- Product owners and business owners defining requirements
- Enterprise architects and platform teams standardizing delivery
- Modernization teams migrating legacy systems

### **Business analysts, product, and proposal teams**

- BAs converting stakeholder input into backlog-ready requirements
- Proposal and capture teams producing artifacts that improve win probability
- Teams that need rapid, clickable prototypes for stakeholder validation

## What can you build with BlueGenAI

BlueGenAI helps you create and maintain the core building blocks of delivery:

- **Requirements packages**

  Actors/roles, user journeys, user stories, acceptance criteria, business rules, edge cases, and traceability matrices.
- **Design artifacts**

  Workflows, data models, screen inventories, permissions models, integration patterns, and technical architectures.
- **Clickable prototypes and UI scaffolding**

  UI flows that accelerate stakeholder feedback and reduce downstream change.
- **Full applications (as appropriate for your program)**

  React frontends, service layers, and database designs, with modern deployment patterns such as containerized delivery (e.g., Docker).
- **Low Code Platform-specific acceleration**

  An AI assistant purpose-built to accelerate delivery on Low Code Platforms such as Tyler Application Platform, ServiceNow, Microsoft Power Apps, Pegasystems, Salesforce, and more.

## **Security, privacy, and compliance**

BlueGenAI is built with public-sector security and compliance expectations in mind.

- **Data protection by design**

  Clear boundaries on what data is used, stored, and retained—aligned to your governance and data-handling requirements.
- **Deployment flexibility**

  Support for hosted and self-hosted/on-premise deployment models to match agency and integrator security postures.
- **Ownership and control**

  You retain ownership of all generated artifacts and outputs, with the ability to export documents and source code for full control.
- **Public-sector contracting alignment**

  Designed to support procurement and compliance realities, including federal use considerations (e.g., FAR/DFARS where applicable).

## **How BlueGenAI fits into your workflow**

BlueGenAI is designed to integrate into how delivery teams already operate—without forcing changes to your established SDLC or governance model.

A typical workflow includes:

1. **Ingest inputs**

   RFPs, SOWs, requirements documents, stakeholder notes, and existing system documentation.
2. **Generate and refine artifacts**

   Convert inputs into requirements, designs, and backlog-ready work items with review cycles and approvals.
3. **Prototype for validation**

   Create rapid UI and workflow prototypes to confirm understanding before expensive build phases.
4. **Build and integrate**

   Produce implementation-ready scaffolding and integration contracts for engineering execution.
5. **Operate and evolve**

   Apply the same structure to enhancements, change requests, and continuous modernization.
